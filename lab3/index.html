Star Wars Saga

Star Wars is a science fiction franchise that takes place in a galaxy far, far away. The franchise began with the release of the original film, Star Wars (later retitled Star Wars: Episode IV - A New Hope), on May 25, 1977. It quickly became a worldwide pop culture phenomenon, spawning multiple sequels, prequels, spin-off films, animated TV shows, books, comics, video games, and more.
